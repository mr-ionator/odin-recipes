QUICK CHICKEN PICCATA

  • PREP:10 mins
  • COOK:15 mins
  • TOTAL:25 mins
  • SERVINGS:4
  • YIELD:4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 skinless,boneless chicken breast halves
  • cayenne pepper,or to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • all-purpose flour for dredging
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon capers drained
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoon cold unsalted butter,cut in 1/4-inch slices
  • 2 tablespoons fresh italian parsely,chopped

DIRECTIONS

  1. Step 1

    2. Place chicken breasts between 2 layers of plastic wrap and pound to about 1/2-inch thick

  2. Step 2

    3. Season both sides of chicken breasts with cayenne, salt, and black pepper; dredge lightly in flour and shake off any excess.

  3. Step 3

    4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in the pan, reduce heat to medium, and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes per side; remove to a plate.

  4. Step 4

    5. Cook capers in reserved oil, smashing them lightly to release brine, until warmed though, about 30 seconds.

  5. Step 5

    6. Pour white wine into skillet. Scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the pan with a wooden spoon. Cook until reduced by half, about 2 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Stir lemon juice, water, and butter into the reduced wine mixture; cook and stir continuously to form a thick sauce, about 2 minutes. Reduce heat to low and stir parsley through the sauce.

  7. Step 7

    8. Return chicken breasts to the pan cook until heated through, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve with sauce spooned over the top.
